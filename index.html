About Us

At Taxidi, we understand the unique challenges that come with moving to a new city for university, an internship, or a new job. As university students and interns ourselves, we experienced firsthand the difficulties of navigating a new place without reliable transportation. Public transport, especially in California, often fell short of our needs, and renting a car was simply too expensive.

Driven by our personal experiences, we set out to create a solution that would help others in similar situations. Using the combined expertise of a computer engineering background and business & data science from UC Berkeley, we embarked on a mission to provide affordable, reliable, and seamless car leasing options.

We meticulously select and maintain a fleet of used cars that are both dependable and budget-friendly. Our technical skills enable us to find the best vehicles at the best prices, ensuring our customers get the most value for their money. Our technology-driven approach also ensures a seamless process, from booking to return, making car leasing hassle-free and efficient.

Whether you need a car for a weekend getaway, a semester, or an internship, we have flexible and affordable leasing options to suit your needs. At Taxidi, we pride ourselves on offering a seamless and enjoyable rental experience. Our commitment to exceptional customer service means we’re always here to support you, ensuring you have the freedom to explore, travel, and achieve your goals without the stress of unreliable transportation.

Join us at Taxidi and experience the difference of a company that genuinely cares about your journey.
